Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Solar PV Calc ("we", "our", "us") is operated by KALE GES ENERJI URETIM LIMITED SIRKETI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. This data is stored securely via Firebase Authentication.

Calculation Data: When you run a solar analysis, we store the location coordinates, system parameters, and results associated with your account in Firebase Firestore.

We do NOT collect:

  • Your precise real-time location (only coordinates you manually select on the map)
  • Payment or credit card information (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Contacts, photos, or any other personal data from your device

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the solar analysis service
  • To store your calculation history for your convenience
  • To manage your account and subscription status
  • To improve the accuracy and features of the application

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore (Google): For secure account management and cloud data storage. Data stored in EU (europe-west1) servers.
  • NASA POWER API: We send selected coordinates to retrieve solar irradiance data. No personal information is sent.
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management. All payment processing is handled by Apple.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Profile > Account Settings. When you delete your account, all personal data and calculation history is permanently removed. This complies with GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure).

7. Children's Privacy

Solar PV Calc is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights (GDPR/KVKK)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and export your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Contact us at info@kaleges.com to exercise these rights.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated date.
