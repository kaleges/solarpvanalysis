4. Accuracy of Results

The calculations provided by the App are estimates based on:

  • Historical solar irradiance data from NASA POWER (typical meteorological year)
  • Simplified PV production models with assumed system losses
  • User-provided financial parameters (system cost, electricity tariff, etc.)

Actual solar energy production may vary significantly due to local weather variations, shading, panel quality, installation specifics, inverter efficiency, grid limitations, soiling, snow coverage, and maintenance practices.
